# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This project covers contested and consequential territory — AI, labor, consciousness, political economy. Disagreement about the arguments is expected and welcome. What is not welcome is treating disagreement as an occasion for hostility.

Contributors, educators, and students who participate in this project's Discussions, issues, and PRs are expected to engage with ideas rather than people. Critique the argument, not the person making it.

## Expected Behavior

- Engage with intellectual honesty — steelman positions before dismissing them
- Welcome corrections and acknowledge when you are wrong
- Distinguish between factual claims and editorial judgments
- Treat everyone's capacity to reason seriously, regardless of background or credential

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, harassment, or sustained hostility toward any participant
- Dismissing contributions on the basis of identity rather than argument
- Spam, self-promotion unrelated to the project's educational purpose
- Deliberate misrepresentation of others' positions

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub Issues, Discussions, PRs, and any other forum associated with this repository.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported by contacting the project maintainer via GitHub. Reports will be reviewed and responded to. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from the project.

---

This project is committed to being a place where serious intellectual engagement about AI and its implications can happen — across institutions, disciplines, and points of view.
